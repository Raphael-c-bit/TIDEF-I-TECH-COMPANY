Course Overview

Our Graphics Design Mastery course is designed to transform beginners into skilled designers capable of creating professional visuals for both print and digital media. You'll learn industry-standard tools and techniques used by professional designers worldwide.

Through hands-on projects and real-world design challenges, you'll gain practical experience with CorelDraw, Canva, PosterMyWall, and essential design resources like Remove.bg and Pinterest for inspiration.

By the end of this course, you'll have a professional portfolio showcasing your design skills and the confidence to take on freelance projects or pursue a career in graphic design.

Tools & Resources You'll Master

CorelDraw

Professional vector graphics editor for logo design, illustrations, and print materials.

Canva

User-friendly design platform for creating social media graphics, presentations, and marketing materials.
